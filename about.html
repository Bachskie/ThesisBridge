ThesisBridge was founded in Nijmegen to solve a critical challenge: connecting brilliant university students with companies seeking fresh perspectives and innovative research.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 in the heart of Nijmegen, Netherlands, ThesisBridge emerged from a simple observation: talented students were struggling to find meaningful thesis projects, while innovative companies were missing out on academic talent and fresh research perspectives.

As a university city with Radboud University and HAN University of Applied Sciences, Nijmegen is home to thousands of ambitious students. Yet, the traditional process of finding thesis opportunities was fragmented, inefficient, and often relied on personal connections.

We built ThesisBridge to democratize access to research opportunities and create a seamless platform where students and companies can find their perfect match. Today, we're proud to serve the entire Netherlands and beyond, facilitating partnerships that drive innovation and advance careers.
